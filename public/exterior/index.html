Florida Exterior Home Improvement Cost Guides

Exterior work in Florida faces challenges that contractors in other states rarely deal with — relentless UV exposure, salt air corrosion along the coast, hurricane-season scheduling restrictions, and afternoon thunderstorms from May through October that can shut down a job site with 30 minutes’ notice.

These conditions affect both pricing and material choices. A fence that lasts 20 years in Georgia might last 10 in coastal Broward County. Exterior paint that holds up fine in the Midwest can chalk and fade within 3 years in direct South Florida sun. Our guides cover the materials and methods that actually perform in this climate, with pricing specific to your market.
