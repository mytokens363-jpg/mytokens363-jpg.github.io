Florida Pool Cost Guides

Florida has more residential pools than any other state, and the combination of year-round sun, salt air, and acidic rain means pool maintenance and renovation costs are a constant for homeowners. Resurfacing a pool that sits in direct Broward County sun is needed every 7-10 years compared to 10-15 in less intense climates. Pool pumps run longer here, which means they fail sooner — and the variable-speed pump requirements now mandated by Florida code add to replacement costs.

Our pool guides cover real installed pricing for your Florida market, from inground pool installation to screen enclosure replacement. Each guide includes county permit requirements, the CPC (Certified Pool Contractor) license verification you should check, and the seasonal timing that gets you the best pricing from local contractors.
