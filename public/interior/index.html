Florida Interior Remodeling Cost Guides

Interior remodeling in Florida follows its own rules. Tile and vinyl plank dominate over hardwood because they handle humidity better. Kitchen remodels in South Florida run 15-20% above national averages due to higher labor rates and stricter building code requirements for electrical and plumbing rough-ins. And every project that touches plumbing or electrical requires permits that some contractors conveniently “forget” to mention.

Our interior guides give you real pricing for your Florida market — not national averages that don’t reflect what contractors in Miami, Tampa, or Jacksonville actually charge. Each guide covers materials that perform in Florida’s climate, permit requirements for your county, and the specific contractor licenses needed for the job.
