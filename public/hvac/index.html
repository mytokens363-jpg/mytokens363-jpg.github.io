Florida HVAC Cost Guides

Your air conditioner is the hardest-working appliance in your Florida home. It runs 8-10 months per year, battles 90%+ humidity, and fights salt air corrosion if you’re within 15 miles of the coast. The average Florida AC unit lasts 10-15 years — significantly less than the 15-20 year lifespan manufacturers quote for moderate climates.

That means AC replacement is one of the most common and most expensive home projects in the state. Our HVAC guides cover real installed pricing for your specific Florida market, including the SEER2 efficiency ratings now required by federal standards, county permit requirements, and the CAC (Class A Air Conditioning) or CMC contractor license you should verify before hiring.
